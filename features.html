Game Features

Playable Characters

Warrior

A balanced character with high health and moderate damage. Perfect for beginners.

  • Starting Health: 100
  • Starting Damage: 10
  • Special Ability: Shield Block

Archer

A ranged specialist with high damage but lower health. Great for experienced players.

  • Starting Health: 80
  • Starting Damage: 15
  • Special Ability: Rapid Fire

Mage

A powerful spellcaster with area damage abilities. Challenging but rewarding.

  • Starting Health: 70
  • Starting Damage: 20
  • Special Ability: Fireball

Rogue

A fast and agile character with high mobility. Perfect for hit-and-run tactics.

  • Starting Health: 90
  • Starting Damage: 12
  • Special Ability: Dash

Weapons & Power-ups

Basic Weapons

  • Sword - Close range melee weapon
  • Bow - Long range projectile weapon
  • Staff - Magic-based weapon
  • Dagger - Fast melee weapon

Evolved Weapons

  • Greatsword - Enhanced sword with area damage
  • Crossbow - Powerful bow with piercing shots
  • Archmage Staff - Advanced staff with elemental effects
  • Assassin's Blade - Deadly dagger with critical hits

Game Modes

Classic Mode

The original endless survival experience. Fight waves of increasingly difficult enemies and try to survive as long as possible.

Boss Rush

Face off against powerful boss monsters in quick succession. Perfect for testing your build and skills.

Challenge Mode

Special challenges with unique rules and conditions. Complete them to earn exclusive rewards.

Daily Run

A new challenge every day with special modifiers. Compete with other players on the global leaderboard.

Progression System

Character Progression

Unlock new characters and abilities by completing achievements and challenges. Each character has unique starting weapons and special abilities.

Weapon Evolution

Max out your weapons to unlock their evolved forms, which provide unique and powerful effects.

Achievements

Complete various in-game challenges to earn achievements and special rewards.

Regular Updates

We're constantly working on new content and improvements. Recent updates include:

  • New character: The Necromancer
  • Additional weapon evolutions
  • New game mode: Survival Arena
  • Quality of life improvements
  • Bug fixes and balance adjustments
