About Monster Survivors

Our Story

Monster Survivors was born from a passion for creating engaging, challenging, and endlessly replayable games. Our team of developers wanted to create a game that combined the thrill of survival with the strategic depth of roguelite games.

The game's concept evolved from classic survival horror games but with a unique twist - instead of hiding from monsters, players must face them head-on, growing stronger with each wave of enemies they defeat.

Development Journey

The development of Monster Survivors began in early 2023, with a small team of passionate developers working to create something special. We focused on creating a game that would be both accessible to newcomers and challenging for experienced players.

Key milestones in our development include:

  • Initial prototype development
  • Character and weapon system implementation
  • Procedural generation system for power-ups
  • Multiple character classes and abilities
  • Public beta testing and feedback integration

Our Mission

At Monster Survivors, our mission is to create engaging, high-quality games that bring joy to players worldwide. We believe in making games that are both entertaining and challenging, where every playthrough offers a unique experience.

We're committed to:

  • Regular updates and new content
  • Listening to player feedback
  • Maintaining a fair and balanced gameplay experience
  • Creating an inclusive gaming community
