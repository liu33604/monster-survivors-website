About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping survival action game where your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible against relentless hordes of monsters. As time progresses, enemies become stronger and more numerous, testing your skills and strategy.

With its roguelite elements, every playthrough offers a unique experience. Unlock new characters with special abilities, discover powerful items, and experiment with different builds to maximize your survival time.

How to Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Level Up

Collect experience by defeating monsters. When you level up, choose from random power-ups to enhance your abilities.

Survive

Avoid getting overwhelmed by enemies. Collect health items when possible and strategically move to create space between you and the monsters.

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from several unique characters, each with their own starting weapons and special abilities.

Weapon Evolution

Combine weapons and passive items to create evolved, more powerful versions with enhanced effects.

Time-Based Events

Face challenging boss fights and special events at specific time intervals for greater rewards.

Achievements

Complete various in-game challenges to unlock achievements and special rewards.
