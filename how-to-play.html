How to Play Monster Survivors

Basic Controls

Movement

  • W or ↑ - Move Up
  • S or ↓ - Move Down
  • A or ← - Move Left
  • D or → - Move Right

Game Actions

  • Space - Pause Game
  • ESC - Open Menu
  • Mouse Click - Select Options

Game Mechanics

Character Movement

Your character moves automatically in the direction you press. Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate through the map while avoiding enemies.

Combat System

Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies. Focus on positioning yourself strategically to maximize damage while avoiding enemy attacks.

Leveling Up

Defeat enemies to gain experience points. When you level up, you'll be presented with three random power-ups to choose from. Choose wisely to build your character's strength.

Power-ups and Items

Common Power-ups

  • Attack Speed - Increases your attack frequency
  • Damage - Boosts your damage output
  • Movement Speed - Makes you move faster
  • Health - Increases your maximum health
  • Experience Gain - Helps you level up faster

Item Evolution

When you max out certain power-ups, they can evolve into more powerful versions. For example, maxing out the basic attack can evolve into a more powerful weapon.

Advanced Tips

1. Focus on movement and positioning - staying alive is more important than dealing damage

2. Prioritize power-ups that complement your current build

3. Keep an eye on the minimap to avoid getting surrounded

4. Save your ultimate abilities for boss fights or when you're in trouble

5. Experiment with different character classes to find your preferred playstyle
